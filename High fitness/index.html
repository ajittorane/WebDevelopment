About GYM

HI Fitness Studio, established on August 1, 2018, is a premier fitness center located in Old Panvel, Maharashtra. Operating as a proprietorship firm, the studio is strategically situated at Plot No. 148, 3rd Floor, Office No. 3, Shree Sahayya Yuforiya, V B Phadke Road, MCCH Society, Old Panvel, Maharashtra, 410206.
